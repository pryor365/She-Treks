Top five tips

  • If a man wants to take a picture with you, know that it may not be in an innocent way.
  • Men staring can be annoying. Try to ignore it. Don’t give them a reason to keep staring.
  • Don’t let your driver “bring a friend”, weird but they do. If they ask why, I don’t say, “Oh because I’m afraid you’ll rape me and this here is your partner in crime!” That’s just silly but why risk it since everyone warns of it?
  • Be very careful if choosing to Couchsurf in India with men.
  • If you are planning a visit to the street markets consider conservative clothing.

Behavioural Tip

Do not clump all Indians and especially Indian men in the untrustworthy category. As like any country, there are good and bad. It’s important to see what’s happening in the news, but not to let it ruin your mindset.
